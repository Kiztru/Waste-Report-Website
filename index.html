Report Waste Now

Abstract

Marine and water pollution pose a huge threat to aquatic ecosystems. Every year, more waste accumulates on beaches and in our seas. Without a coordinated reporting system, cleanup efforts remain scattered.

Problem Statement

Marine debris piles up on shores and in open water. Manual reporting lacks standardized categorization, slowing response times and inhibiting efficient resource allocation.

Project Benefits

  • Rapid Classification of waste types.
  • Crowdsourced Reporting empowers volunteers.
  • Streamlined Cleanup by notifying authorities.
  • Increased Awareness of pollution hotspots.

System Features

  • Intuitive Web Form for waste input and photo upload.
  • Map Integration with GPS tagging via OpenStreetMap.
  • MySQL Storage for scalable report data.
  • Downloadable Reports in plain-text format.
  • Mobile-Friendly responsive layout.
  • Validation & Security—HTTPS ready.

Supported SDGs

  • SDG 6 – Clean Water & Sanitation
  • SDG 13 – Climate Action
  • SDG 14 – Life Below Water
